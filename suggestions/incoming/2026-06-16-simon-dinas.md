In the first trip listed, Dinas (which is a common Welsh hamlet name) is the
wrong one. It should be inland from Llanbedr. I think it was misled by "Dinas
Camp" instead of "Dinas".

**Applied 2026-06-17:** glossary `places` now maps the family's "Dinas"
campsite references to Dinas Farm, Llanbedr (52.8423, -4.0645).
